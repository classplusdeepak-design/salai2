Cancellation Policy

← Back to Home

At Salai Easy Maths, we aim to provide a reliable learning experience. Please review our cancellation terms before enrolling in any course.

1. Student Cancellation

  • Once a student enrolls in a course, cancellation is not permitted.
  • No refund will be provided after course access has been granted.

2. Institute Cancellation

Salai Easy Maths reserves the right to cancel a course in situations such as:

  • Minimum required enrollment is not achieved
  • Unforeseen circumstances or operational issues

In such cases:

  • A full refund will be provided to the enrolled students.
  • Refunds will be processed through the original payment method as per applicable processing timelines.

For further assistance, please contact our support team.
