Return & Refund Policy

← Back to Home

At Salai Easy Maths, we strive to provide quality courses and a smooth learning experience. Please review our refund policy before purchasing any course.

1. No Refund Policy

  • All course purchases are non-refundable once access to the course has been granted.
  • Students are advised to review course details before completing the payment.

2. Exceptional Cases

A refund request may be considered only in the following situations:

  • Duplicate payment made accidentally
  • Technical issues from our side that prevent access to the purchased course

3. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 working days.
  • Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method used during the transaction.

For any refund-related queries, please contact our support team.
