Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join Salai Easy Maths courses?

Salai Easy Maths courses are suitable for all GATE, ESE, PSUs, SSC JE, AE/JE and BARC aspirants, including college students, graduates, and working professionals. Whether you are a beginner or repeating the exam, our structured programs help you prepare effectively at every stage.

Are classes live or recorded?

We offer both live and recorded classes. Live sessions ensure interaction and discipline, while recorded lectures help you revise anytime. This combination allows flexible and consistent learning without missing any important topic.

Do you provide test series?

Yes, we provide subject-wise and full-length test series based on the actual exam pattern. Each test includes detailed solutions and performance analysis to help you improve accuracy, speed, and overall exam performance.

Is there any mentorship support?

Yes, students receive continuous mentorship, including doubt-solving sessions and preparation guidance. Our mentors help you stay consistent, improve weak areas, and follow the right strategy throughout your preparation journey.

Can beginners join?

Absolutely. Our courses start from basic concepts and gradually move to advanced levels. Even beginners can build strong fundamentals and confidently prepare for GATE and other competitive exams.

How long will I get access to the course?

You get access to live classes and recorded lectures for a sufficient duration, covering your full preparation cycle. This ensures enough time for revision, practice, and concept strengthening.  

Are study materials and PDFs included in the course?

Yes, all courses include structured study materials and PDFs designed for quick revision and concept clarity. These are aligned with the latest syllabus and exam trends for effective preparation.
