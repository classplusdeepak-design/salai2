Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join Salai Easy Maths courses?

Salai Easy Maths courses are designed for UG, PG, Polytechnic, Engineering, SET, CSIR, GATE, and TNPSC aspirants. Whether you are a college student, graduate, working professional, beginner, or repeat candidate, our structured programs provide the guidance, practice, and strategy needed to succeed at every stage of your preparation.

Are classes live or recorded?

We offer both live and recorded classes to ensure a complete learning experience. Live sessions provide interaction, guidance, and accountability, while recorded lectures allow you to learn and revise at your convenience. This flexible approach helps you stay consistent and never miss an important topic.

Do you provide Free Material?

Yes, we provide subject-wise and full-length free mock tests designed according to the latest exam patterns. Each test comes with detailed solutions and performance analysis to help you identify weaknesses, improve accuracy, increase speed, and maximize your exam performance.

Is there any mentorship support?

Yes, students receive continuous mentorship, personalized preparation guidance, and dedicated doubt-solving sessions. Our mentors help you stay consistent, strengthen weak areas, and follow the right strategy to achieve your goals with confidence.

Can beginners join?

Absolutely. Our courses are designed to take you from basic concepts to advanced problem-solving in a structured manner. Even if you are a beginner, you can build a strong mathematical foundation and confidently prepare for TRB and other competitive examinations.

How long will I get access to the course?

You get access to both live classes and recorded lectures for a duration that covers your complete preparation cycle. This gives you ample time for learning, revision, practice, and strengthening concepts to ensure exam readiness.  

Are study materials and PDFs included in the course?

Yes, all courses include well-structured study materials and concise PDF notes designed for quick revision and conceptual clarity. These resources are aligned with the latest syllabus and exam patterns to support effective and focused preparation.
